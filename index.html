Abstract摘要 · academic form

Problem. Astrological language is often reduced either to entertainment, personality typing, or deterministic prediction. In those forms, it becomes a moral-language machine: a coordinate is treated as a character verdict, reception impact is misattributed to the receiver, and the receiver's self-description is trapped inside externally imposed categories. Approach. Starchart Ethical Language reframes the natal chart as a reception-coordinate language rather than a predictive causal system. Its core claim is that the ethical weight of a speech-act is not determined by the sentence alone, but by the relation between the speech-act and the receiver's reception architecture. The framework proceeds by first demolishing four moral-language mechanisms, then reconstructing chart language through four legitimacy criteria: specificity sufficient for falsifiability, receiver-side self-use, f-input rather than f-output status, and cross-translatability with other coordinate languages. Contribution. S-E-L contributes a receiver-side ethics of address for speech-act theory, a methodological bridge between symbolic coordinate systems and trauma-informed reception analysis, and a third-school position inside NODE 20 alongside sender-side V-L-E and medium-side E-L-P. It is a philosophical-ethical research artifact, not clinical advice or empirical validation of astrological prediction.

问题。星盘语言常被压缩为娱乐、性格类型学或决定论预测。在这些形式中，它会变成道德语言机器：坐标被当作性格判决，接收冲击被错置给接收者，接收者的自我描述被锁进外部强加的分类。方法。星盘伦理语言将出生星盘重构为接收坐标语言，而非预测性因果系统。它的核心主张是：言语动作的伦理重量不是由句子本身单独决定，而是由言语动作与接收者接收架构之间的关系决定。框架先拆解四种道德语言机制，再用四条合法性判据重建星盘语言：具体度足以证伪、接收者本人使用、作为 f-input 而非 f-output、可与其他坐标语言交叉翻译。贡献。S-E-L 为言语行为理论提供接收端伦理学，为象征坐标系统与创伤知情的接收分析提供方法桥梁，并在 NODE 20 中与 V-L-E、E-L-P 并列成为第三学派。它是哲学-伦理研究物，不是临床建议，也不是对占星预测的经验验证。

Boundary Statement边界声明 · what this discipline does not claim
Not pop astrology · 不是流行占星

Starchart Ethical Language uses the chart as a coordinate-system language, not as a character-verdict instrument or fate-prediction system. This framework studies how astrological vocabulary functions as a coordinate language for reception ethics, not whether astrology has predictive causal validity.

星盘伦理语言把星盘当作坐标系语言使用，不把它当性格判决工具或命运预测系统。本框架研究星盘语言如何作为接收伦理的坐标语言运作，而不是主张占星具有预测性因果效力。

Not deterministic prediction · 不是决定论预测

Reception architecture varies, is real, and has ethical consequences — and remains updatable by experience. The chart is a map of architecture, not a prison of identity. Coordinate positions function as f-input, never as f-output predicting behavior.

接收架构有差异、是真实的、有伦理后果——并且随经验可更新。星盘是架构的地图，不是身份的监狱。坐标位置作为 f 的输入 使用，绝不作为输出预测行为。

Not clinical advice · 不是临床建议

"Trauma as empirical apparatus" names a methodological position. It is not psychiatric diagnosis, therapeutic prescription, or substitute for clinical care.

“创伤作为经验仪器”命名的是方法论位置。它不是精神医学诊断、不是治疗处方、不替代临床照护。
