道德语言幻觉的四个机制Four Mechanisms of the Moral-Language Illusion

The starchart itself is a coordinate system — a record of geometric positions at the moment of birth. The coordinate system carries no ethicality of its own. The false form resides not in the coordinate system, but in the manner of its use. When the chart is used as moral language, four identifiable mechanisms operate. Each builds measurable wreckage in the receiver's body.

星盘本身是一个坐标系——出生时刻的几何位置记录。坐标系本身无伦理性。伪形不在坐标系里，伪形在它被使用的方式里。当星盘被当作道德语言使用时，有四个可识别的机制。每一个都在接收者身体里建造可测量的残骸。

Character Verdict性格判决

表面形式：描述。实际功能：判决。把坐标当作性格证据，把性格当作行为合法性的判据。 Surface form: description. Actual function: verdict. Treats coordinate position as evidence for character property, treats character property as a verdict on behavioral legitimacy.
